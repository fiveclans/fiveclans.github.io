Five Clans Online has closed.

It is with great dismay that I must announce the closure of Five Clans Online.

Unfortunately we are unable to continue to dedicate both the time and energy that is required to meet player expectations. It has always been our goal to provide a fair, balanced, and enjoyable experience for everyone. Even if there are many things I wish we could have done differently, I believe that goal was fulfilled.

I want to give a sincere thank you to everyone who has supported us throughout the years. This is a game that truly thrives on its community, through both players and GM's. Without your dedication we would have never made it this far.

Farewell!

(GM)Five
